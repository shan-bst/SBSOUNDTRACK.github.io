Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo is a Grammy-winning singer-songwriter and actress known for her emotionally raw lyrics, powerful vocals, and storytelling ability. Bursting onto the music scene with her record-breaking debut single "drivers license," Olivia has become a voice of her generation, blending pop and indie influences with themes of heartbreak, self-discovery, and resilience.
