Allan Walker

Alan Walker is a British-Norwegian DJ, record producer, and songwriter, renowned for his signature blend of electronic music and cinematic soundscapes. Rising to fame with his global hit "Faded," Walker has captivated audiences worldwide with his distinct sound and iconic hoodie-and-mask persona.
