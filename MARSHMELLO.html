Marshmello

Marshmello is a globally renowned DJ and producer celebrated for his signature electronic music and iconic marshmallow-shaped helmet. Blending future bass, pop, and EDM, Marshmello has created chart-topping tracks and collaborated with artists across genres, captivating audiences worldwide with his uplifting beats and creative style.
