Radiohead

Radiohead is a critically acclaimed English rock band known for their experimental approach to music, blending alternative rock, electronic, and art rock elements. With groundbreaking albums like *OK Computer* and *Kid A*, they have influenced countless artists and are celebrated for their thought-provoking lyrics, innovative soundscapes, and dynamic performances.
