Second Hand Serenade

Secondhand Serenade is an acoustic rock project by singer-songwriter John Vesely, known for heartfelt lyrics and emotive melodies. Combining raw vocals with stripped-down instrumentation, his music captures themes of love, heartbreak, and vulnerability, resonating deeply with fans of emotional and introspective ballads.
