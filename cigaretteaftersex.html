Cigarette After Sex

Cigarettes After Sex is an American ambient pop band known for their dreamy, atmospheric soundscapes and introspective lyrics. Fronted by Greg Gonzalez, the band has captivated listeners with their minimalist melodies, ethereal vocals, and themes of love, longing, and nostalgia.
